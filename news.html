Rural Education Enhancement Program

Kigali Region Jan 2024 - Dec 2025 1,200 Beneficiaries

This comprehensive program aims to improve educational infrastructure and learning outcomes in 15 rural schools. We're building new classrooms, providing modern learning materials, training teachers, and establishing computer labs to bridge the digital divide.

Project Completion 75%

Community Health Initiative

Eastern Province Mar 2024 - Jun 2025 3,500 Beneficiaries

Expanding access to quality healthcare through mobile clinics, health education programs, and maternal care services in underserved communities. The project includes training community health workers and establishing health monitoring systems.

Project Completion 60%
In Progress

Youth Skills & Entrepreneurship Program

Multiple Districts May 2024 - Dec 2025 800 Beneficiaries

Empowering unemployed youth with marketable skills through vocational training in carpentry, tailoring, mechanics, and digital marketing. The program includes business development training and startup capital for successful graduates.

Project Completion 45%
Completed

Clean Water Access Project

Northern Province Jan 2024 - Nov 2024 2,000 Beneficiaries

Successfully installed 12 water wells and filtration systems providing clean drinking water to rural communities. The project also included hygiene education workshops and the formation of water management committees.

Project Completion 100%
In Progress

Women's Economic Empowerment

Southern Province Jul 2024 - Sep 2025 500 Beneficiaries

Supporting women through cooperative formation, microfinance access, business training, and market linkages. The program focuses on agricultural value addition and handicraft production for sustainable income generation.

Project Completion 35%
