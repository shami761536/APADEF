Where We Work

APDFE operates in four of the world's most challenging humanitarian contexts‚Äîplaces where conflict, poverty, and displacement intersect to create urgent needs for women and children. Our presence in these countries is not symbolic‚Äîit's operational, deeply rooted, and community-driven. We go where others cannot or will not go.

Our Primary Hub

Central African Republic

The epicenter of our work. CAR has endured decades of instability, with ongoing armed violence affecting millions‚Äîparticularly women and girls who face extreme rates of gender-based violence. We operate in Bangui and surrounding provinces, focusing on:

  • GBV response & survivor support
  • Mental health & psychosocial services
  • Child protection in conflict zones
  • Women's economic empowerment

Eastern Provinces

Democratic Republic of Congo
(Eastern DRC)

Active armed conflict in North Kivu, South Kivu, and Ituri has displaced millions and created one of the world's most severe humanitarian catastrophes. We work in the hardest-hit areas providing:

  • Emergency nutrition for malnourished children
  • Trauma healing & mental health support
  • Education for displaced children
  • Peace-building & conflict mediation
28,000+ Reached 12 Communities Since 2020

Why Central Africa?

Central Africa hosts some of the world's longest-running humanitarian crises. These four countries share borders, populations, conflicts, and displacement patterns‚Äîcreating a complex web of interconnected challenges. An integrated regional approach allows us to serve refugees, returnees, and host communities more effectively‚Äîmeeting people wherever they are on their journey from survival to dignity.
